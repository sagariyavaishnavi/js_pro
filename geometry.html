Cube

Volume = l³
Surface Area = 6 * l²

Sphere

Volume = 4/3 * π * r³
Surface Area = 4 * π * r²

Cylinder

Volume = π * r² * h
Surface Area = 2 * π * r * (r + h)

Cone

Volume = 1/3 * π * r² * h
Surface Area = π * r * (r + h)

Ellipsoid

Volume = (4/3) * π * r1 * r2 * r3
Surface Area ≈ 4 * π * ((r1^p * r2^p + r1^p * r3^p + r2^p * r3^p) / 3)^(1/p), where p ≈ 1.6075

Pyramid

Volume = 1/3 * b² * h
Surface Area = b² + 2 * b * h

Tetrahedron

Volume = (edge³) / (6 * √2)

Hemisphere

Volume = 2/3 * π * r³
Surface Area = 3 * π * r²

Torus

Volume = 2 * π² * r * tube r²
Surface Area = 4 * π² * r * tube r
